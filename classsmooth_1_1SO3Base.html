Detailed Description

template<typename _Derived>
class smooth::SO3Base< _Derived >

Base class for SO3 Lie group types.

Internally represented as a member of \( \mathbb{S}^3 \) (unit quaternions).

Memory layout

  • Group: \( \mathbf{x} = [q_x, q_y, q_z, q_w] \) (same as Eigen quaternion).
  • Tangent: \( \mathbf{a} = [\omega_x, \omega_y, \omega_z] \)

Constraints

  • Group: \(q_x^2 + q_y^2 + q_z^2 + q_w^2 = 1 \)
  • Tangent: \( -\pi < \omega_x, \omega_y, \omega_z \leq \pi \)

Lie group matrix form

3x3 rotation matrix

Lie algebra matrix form

\[ \mathbf{a}^\wedge = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & -\omega_z & \omega_y \\ \omega_z & 0 & -\omega_x \\ -\omega_y & \omega_x & 0 \\ \end{bmatrix} \]

Parameters
i1,i2,i3euler angle axis convention ( \(0=x, 1=y, 2=z\)). Default values correspond to ZYX rotation.

Returned angles a1, a2, a3 are s.t. rotation is described by \( Rot_{i_1}(a_1) * Rot_{i_2}(a_2) * Rot_{i_3}(a_3). \) where \( Rot_i(a) \) rotates an angle \(a\) around the \(i\):th axis.
